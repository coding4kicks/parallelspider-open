About

Parallel Spider is a tool for website analysis and comparison. The system allows individuals with non-technical backgrounds to perform advanced data analysis tasks on websites: What words are used? How are those words used? What sites are linked to? How do two or more sites compare? and more…

We can’t tell you exactly how to use Parallel Spider. The answer to that question depends upon what interests you and what you want to discover. Parallel Spider is designed to be a general tool. A tool to help you reach insights into the worlds that matter to you on that wide web called the Internet.

Plot Points

 

Fast

Instead of one computer to download and analyze the data, Parallel Spider uses 20 computers for each crawl. Why don’t we use even more? To be a good citizen on the net, we only download 20 pages per second from the websites being analyzed. This download rate still allows the system to analyze over a thousand pages per minute, and is much better than a single computer solution. A five-thousand-page site can be completely analyzed in less than 5 minutes.

 

Easy

Just select the analysis options you want and the sites you wish to crawl. No need to use XPATH or CSS selectors unless you want. No need to download anything. Just a few clicks and your off on your very own crawl. We even provide visualizations to help you understand the data and to help you convey that understanding to others.

 

Free

Parallel Spider has a free tier of usage. Learn the system, see if you like it, or just use it for a few things and be done with it. Beyond the beginning free tier, prices are cheap. Analyze 10,000 pages for less than a dollar. Different options cost pennies. Only pay for what you use.
